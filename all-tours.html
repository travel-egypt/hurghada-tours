Safari Program - Full Day Adventure in the Desert

Trip Overview

Join us on an unforgettable desert adventure through the Eastern Sahara. Discover the authentic Bedouin lifestyle, ride quad bikes, enjoy a camel ride, and witness a breathtaking desert sunset with a delicious BBQ dinner.

Itinerary

  • 1:00 PM – Pickup from your hotel
  • 2:00 PM – Quad Biking adventure through the sand dunes
  • 3:00 PM – Buggy Ride (optional)
  • 3:30 PM – Bedouin Village visit and culture experience
  • 4:00 PM – Camel Ride and scenic photos
  • 5:00 PM – Bedouin Tea & Shisha (optional)
  • 6:00 PM – Sunset BBQ Dinner
  • 7:00 PM – Oriental Show with Tanoura, belly dance, fire show
  • 8:00 PM – Return to your hotel

What's Included

  • Hotel transfers
  • Quad bike, camel ride
  • Bedouin village tour
  • BBQ dinner + soft drinks
  • Oriental show
  • English-speaking guide

Cairo Day Trip - Explore the Pyramids

Trip Overview

A magical journey from Hurghada to Cairo where you’ll discover the Great Pyramids of Giza, the Sphinx, and the treasures of the Egyptian Museum.

Itinerary

  • Early morning pickup from your hotel
  • Visit the Egyptian Museum with guided tour
  • Lunch at a local restaurant
  • Explore Giza Pyramids, the Sphinx, and Valley Temple
  • Shopping at papyrus and perfume bazaars
  • Drive back to Hurghada

What's Included

  • Transfers by air-conditioned vehicle
  • Entrance fees to all sites
  • Lunch and bottled water
  • Licensed Egyptologist guide
