🏜️ Hurghada Desert Safari – Full-Day Program

Experience the thrill of Egypt’s Eastern Desert like never before!

🌄 Tour Highlights & Full Program:

  • Hotel Pickup (12:30 PM): Pickup from your Hurghada hotel in an A/C vehicle.
  • Quad Biking Adventure: Ride through the desert dunes for 45 minutes.
  • Camel Ride & Bedouin Village: Visit a Bedouin camp and experience traditional life.
  • BBQ Dinner: Open-buffet BBQ meal with soft drinks.
  • Sunset & Stargazing: Watch the desert sunset and gaze at the stars.
  • Return (7:00 PM): Back to your hotel with unforgettable memories.

📍 Duration: ~6 hours | 📅 Available Daily

🏛️ Cairo Day Trip – Giza Pyramids & Museum

Witness one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World in a day full of discovery and culture.

🚐 Tour Program:

  • Early Pickup: Depart from Hurghada by bus (air-conditioned with guide).
  • Giza Pyramids: Guided tour of the Pyramids of Giza and the Sphinx.
  • Lunch: Local restaurant meal with Egyptian dishes.
  • Egyptian Museum: Explore artifacts including treasures of King Tutankhamun.
  • Shopping: Visit local bazaars for souvenirs.
  • Return: Back to Hurghada by evening.

📍 Duration: ~18 hours | 📅 Available Daily

🛶 Luxor & Aswan Tour – Ancient Temples & Nile

Step into the heart of Ancient Egypt in the world’s greatest open-air museum.

🏛️ Tour Program:

  • Valley of the Kings: Visit tombs of legendary pharaohs.
  • Karnak Temple: Marvel at massive columns and hieroglyphs.
  • Hatshepsut Temple: Learn about Egypt’s female pharaoh.
  • Boat Ride (optional): Short sailing trip on the Nile.
  • Lunch: Traditional Egyptian meal in Luxor.
  • Return: Back to Hurghada in the evening.

📍 Duration: ~17 hours | 📅 Available 3x/week
